TRELLO-PANDADOCS INTEGRATION

ABOUT

Due to the current global situation, a great number of companies have opted for remote work. This has caused an increased amount of work for HR departments, specially handling documents, like permits. To make their work easier we designed a tool that integrates the list-making application Trello and the document management app Pandadocs.
